Limerick Coding Club

Code. Create. Collaborate.

Welcome to the Limerick Coding Club, a vibrant hub where coding enthusiasts of all levels come together to innovate, learn, and grow. In our meetups, we dive into coding projects, share meals to fuel our brainstorming sessions, and collaboratively learn new skills through workshops led by tech experts. It's not just about coding; it's about building a community where members collaborate on projects, engage in creative problem-solving, and contribute to the wider community through tech. Whether you're looking to improve your coding skills, network with fellow tech enthusiasts, or just explore the world of technology, the Limerick Coding Club is your gateway to a world of possibility and innovation.

"Launching the Limerick Coding Club has been a journey of community, innovation, and shared growth, transforming our passion for technology into a beacon of creativity and collaboration." - Muhammad Uquaily - Founder of the Club
