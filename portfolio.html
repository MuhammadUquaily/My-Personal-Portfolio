About

My name is Muhammad Uquaily, and at the young age of 13, I have embarked on a remarkable journey into the world of software development with Codecademy. Currently, I am diligently working through the Full Stack Engineer course, where I am not only learning the technical skills necessary for web development but also developing a deeper appreciation for the craft of coding. My experience with Codecademy has been incredibly empowering; it has opened my eyes to the potential of coding as a tool for creativity and problem-solving.

In addition to my coding endeavors, I have a rich life outside of the digital world. I am passionate about football, a sport that teaches me about teamwork, strategy, and perseverance. Reading is another one of my hobbies, allowing me to explore vast worlds and ideas beyond my immediate experience. Working out is also an integral part of my routine, keeping me physically fit and mentally sharp. Furthermore, I am actively working on building my personal brand on Instagram at @muhammaduquaily, where I share insights into my journey as a young coder, connect with like-minded individuals, and hopefully inspire others to take up coding.

Limerick Coding Club

The Limerick Coding Club is a project that holds a special place in my portfolio. It represents a blend of imagination and technical skill, depicting a fictional coding community based in Limerick, Ireland. This project allowed me to explore the concept of community in the tech world, imagining a place where people come together to share their passion for coding. The website features detailed descriptions of club activities, workshops, and testimonials, including one from its fictional founder, Muhammad Uquaily, aimed at capturing the essence of communal learning and growth in coding.

Form A Story

"Form a Story" was an engaging project from Codecademy that allowed me to dive into the world of HTML forms. This project was particularly interesting because it merged the technical aspect of web development with creative storytelling. Users of the site can input their own words and choices into a form, and a unique story is generated based on their submissions. This project showcased the interactive potential of web development, providing a personalized experience for each user and highlighting the importance of user input in web design.
